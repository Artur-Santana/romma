| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-25-01 | Elevation of Privilege (IDOR) | confirmarPagamentoLocatario | mitigate | Guard 3-hop server-side: parcela→contrato→locatario, comparar locatario.usuario_id === user.id; cross-tenant → 404 (mascaramento) |
| T-25-02 | Tampering | confirmarPagamentoLocatario — parcela.id | mitigate | UUID_RE regex valida formato antes de qualquer query; ID inválido → 400 |
